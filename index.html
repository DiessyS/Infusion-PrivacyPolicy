Privacy Policy

Our app does not collect any personal data. We respect your privacy and ensure that no information is stored or transmitted to external servers.

Our app may access files stored on your device to provide the necessary functionality. All file access is local to your device, meaning no file data is transmitted or shared externally. This ensures your data stays secure and private within your own system.

Data Protection

The app processes and handles your data using the built-in encryption algorithms provided by the Android operating system. This ensures that any data accessed or managed by the app is securely encrypted and decrypted directly on your device, without leaving your system.

Clipboard Usage

Our app may use the clipboard functionality to copy and paste data as per your request. We do not track or store any clipboard content.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the app.
